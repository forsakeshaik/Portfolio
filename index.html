About Me


Hey there! I’m Shaik Shahidh Siddique, a tech enthusiast with a creative twist. My love affair with technology kicked off back in 7th grade when I stumbled upon Blender 3D and started crafting epic renders—think of it as my digital playground. Fast forward to now, and I’ve expanded my creative toolkit to include web design and development, where I mix HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React into some seriously sleek, responsive websites. But I don’t just stop at the front end—I dive into data science and machine learning too, turning raw data into cool insights and solutions. Whether I’m building an anime recommendation system, analyzing sentiment, or making a website come to life, I’m all about blending creativity with tech. I’m also deeply fascinated by Japanese culture, so when I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me lost in a world of anime, manga, or grooving to some city pop. Let’s create something awesome together and make the web a more beautiful place!
